Bypassing YouTube's video checking system

A small research on YouTube content detection.

This entry has the objective of showcasing the result of an investigation performed in YouTube's checking feauture, specifically, how does the platform tackle the detection of content which goes against Terms of Service (ToS).

The main reason behind this research is to see how AI, paired with other detection techniques pair up to perform the task at hand.

It is of upmost importance to note everything explained here has been performed under curiousity viewpoints, by no means does this have the intention to be replicated.
